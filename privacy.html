Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 26, 2025

This privacy policy describes how your data is handled by the Pesture Drawing web application.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No cookies or tracking scripts are used.
  • No usage analytics are recorded.

Use of Third-Party APIs

In the future, Pesture Drawing will use the Pinterest API to allow users to fetch boards and images from their own Pinterest account. When this feature is implemented:

  • You will be asked to grant access via Pinterest’s secure login.
  • Only basic data needed to display your boards and images will be accessed.
  • No data will be stored or shared with third parties.

Security

All data is processed locally in your browser. No files, images, or session data is uploaded or transmitted.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at: hello@pesturedrawing.com
